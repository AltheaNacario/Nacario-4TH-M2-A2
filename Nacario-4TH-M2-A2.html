COLORS OF THE RAINBOW

Red Red is the first color from the rainbow next to orange and opposite to violet. It has the longest wavelength on the visible spectrum of light.

Orange Orange is the colour between yellow and red on the spectrum of visible light. It is a color that oozes with delight.

Yellow Yellow is the color between green and orange on the spectrum of light. It represents joy and optimism, wisdom and creativity, as well as sunshine and the coming of spring.

Green Green is the color between cyan and yellow on the visible spectrum. It may signify fresh starts, expansion, renewal, and abundance.

Blue Blue is the color between violet and cyan on the spectrum of visible light. It can represent serenity, sturdiness, inspiration, wisdom, and reliability.

Indigo Indigo is the color between blue and violet however, sources differ as to its actual position in the electromagnetic spectrum. It is the color of devotion, understanding, fairness, and higher learning.

Violet Violet is the color between blue and invisible ultraviolet. It has the shortest wavelength on the visible spectrum of light.
